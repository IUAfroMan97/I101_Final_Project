The Problem

A new food truck has just opened up in Bloomington and students really like the food that they serve. They make the food quickly and it is relatively cheap. The only problem is that the food truck is having a hard time making their name known since they are new in town and are not very technologically savvy. Because of this, they have asked us to determine how they can connect to with their customers and attract new customers.

Food trucks have existed since the late 1800s, but only recently became popular in large, urban areas. They have been an important part of big cities since the early 2000s. According to proponents of these food trucks, they “activate public space” and “foster a sense of community” (Wessel 2012), but this is not the case with our food truck because the owners are unsure of how to reach out and make lasting connections with their customers.

This problem is not only limited to our food truck, but after some research we have found that most food trucks, especially in the city of Bloomington, do not properly take advantage of technology in order to reach their customers. After examining some big cities and their approach to the issue, we have decided to create a food truck app that will allow greater company-customer interaction.
