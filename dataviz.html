Survey Data : database download

We designed and sent out a survey about food truck use and interest. We asked four questions: if you buy food from trucks, how often, if you follow trucks on social media and if you would be interested in an app that connected you to food trucks.

We gathered information from 30 people. The following is the data we collected in chronological order.

We found that 63% of our respondents buy from food trucks.

78% of the people who buy from food trucks buy less than once a month. 21% buy around once a month and the remaining 1 person buys a few times per month.

Only 6.67% of respondents follow trucks on social media, while 83.33% said they do not. Of the people who do not, 28% want to follow trucks on social media. 10% of our respondents do not have social media. 

60% of our respondents expressed interest in our app. 77.8% of the people interested in the app already buy from food trucks. 36.4% of the people who do not buy from food trucks expressed interest in our app.
